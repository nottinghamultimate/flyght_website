About Flyght

Flyght club is an ultimate frisbee team based in Nottingham, UK established in 2001. We are a friendly and inclusive club that welcomes players of all skill levels, from beginners to experienced athletes. Our mission is to promote the sport of ultimate frisbee and provide a supportive community for players to develop their skills and enjoy the game. We participate in local, regional, and national competitions, and we also organize social events and training sessions throughout the year.

We enter teams into all divisions, Womens, Open, and Mixed, so no matter what you prefer there will always be something for you.
