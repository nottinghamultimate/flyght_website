Flyght Ultimate

Friendly, competitive ultimate frisbee in Nottingham

Flyght Club is a friendly and competitive Ultimate club based in Nottingham, UK. We welcome players of all experience levels, from complete beginners trying the sport for the first time to experienced players looking for competitive opportunities.

Whether you're looking to keep active, meet new people, or compete at a high level, we'd love to have you join us.

The easiest way to get involved is to join our Discord community, where you'll find the latest training information, club news, events, and opportunities to play.

Why Flyght?

Founded in 2001, Flyght Club has spent more than two decades building an inclusive and welcoming Ultimate community in Nottingham.

We regularly enter local, regional, and national competitions across Open, Women's, and Mixed divisions, providing opportunities for players of all ambitions and experience levels. Some of our members have gone on to compete at the highest levels of the sport, including international competition.

Beyond training and tournaments, we organise regular socials, friendly fixtures, and collaborative events with other clubs across the region.

Whether you're aiming to compete, stay active, learn a new sport, or simply meet great people, there's a place for you at Flyght.
