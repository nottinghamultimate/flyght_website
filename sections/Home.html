Flyght Ultimate

Friendly, competitive ultimate frisbee in Nottingham

Flyght Club is a friendly and competitive Ultimate club based in Nottingham, UK. We welcome players of all experience levels, from complete beginners trying the sport for the first time to experienced players looking for competitive opportunities.

Whether you're looking to keep active, meet new people, or compete at a high level, we'd love to have you join us.

The easiest way to get involved is to join our Discord community, where you'll find the latest training information, club news, events, and opportunities to play.
